Cancellation & Refund Policy

Overview

At Codeilm, we strive to provide the best learning experience for our users. However, if you are not satisfied with our services or wish to request a refund, we have outlined our cancellation and refund policies below.

1. Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your subscription or course enrollment at any time. However, please note that cancellation does not guarantee a refund unless it meets the conditions stated in our refund policy.

2. Refund Policy

Refunds are only available under the following conditions:

  • If you cancel your subscription within 7 days of purchase and have not accessed more than 10% of the course content.
  • Refund requests must be made within 7 days of the purchase date by contacting our support team at support@codeilm.com.
  • Once your refund is approved, it will be processed within 7-10 business days and returned to the original payment method.

3. Non-refundable Situations

We do not provide refunds in the following cases:

  • When the user has accessed more than 10% of the course content.
  • In case of late refund requests (i.e., beyond the 7-day refund period).
  • If the course was purchased during promotional periods or at a discounted rate, unless specified otherwise.

4. Changes to the Policy

Codeilm reserves the right to modify or update this cancellation and refund policy at any time. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically to stay informed of any changes.
