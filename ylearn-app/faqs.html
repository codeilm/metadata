Frequently Asked Questions

What is the YLearn App? ▶
YLearn is an educational app that provides resources for students of classes 9 to 12, including quizzes, solutions, and a study timetable feature.
How can I create a study timetable? ▶
Simply go to the Study Timetable section in the app, and use the intuitive interface to plan your day by adding subjects and study hours.
Is YLearn App free? ▶
Yes, the YLearn App is free for all students. We sustain through ads and optional premium features that enhance the experience.
How can I report a problem? ▶
If you encounter any issues, feel free to reach out to us through the Contact Us page. We will get back to you as soon as possible.
Can I access the app without internet? ▶
Some features like quizzes and solutions are available offline, but others require an internet connection for access.
